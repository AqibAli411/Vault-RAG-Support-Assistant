# Internal Memo: Compliance Automation Q4 Priorities

Prepared by: Risk and Controls
Date: September 2026

Q4 priorities include:
1. tuning suspicious-activity alert thresholds to reduce false positives;
2. automating evidence collection for audit trails;
3. improving model governance records for regulatory reviews.

The compliance team requires clear traceability of any AI-generated response to source documents in support workflows.
