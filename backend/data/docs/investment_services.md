# Vault Investment & Portfolio Services

## Account Tiers
Vault offers three account tiers with different capabilities:

### Standard (Free)
- Daily transfer limit: $25,000
- No investment features
- Basic fraud monitoring
- Email support only

### Premium ($19.99/month)
- Daily transfer limit: $50,000
- Access to Vault Portfolios (managed investment accounts)
- Priority fraud monitoring with dedicated analyst
- 24/7 phone and chat support
- Free international wire transfers (up to 5/month)

### Business ($49.99/month)
- Daily transfer limit: $250,000
- Multi-user access with role-based permissions
- API access for accounting integration
- Dedicated relationship manager
- Custom fraud rules and alerts
- Batch payment processing

## Market Data Access
Premium and Business tier members have access to real-time market data including:
- Major index tracking (S&P 500, NASDAQ, DOW)
- Cryptocurrency prices (BTC, ETH, SOL)
- Foreign exchange rates for 30+ currency pairs
- Commodity prices (Gold, Silver, Oil)

All market data is provided for informational purposes only. Vault does not provide investment advice.
