# Vault Digital Banking — Product Changelog

## v3.2.0 — April 2026
- **Voice Banking Pilot**: Limited rollout of voice-activated balance checks and transfers via Vault mobile app
- **Enhanced Fraud Alerts**: Real-time push notifications now include transaction location maps
- **Batch Payments**: Business tier can now upload CSV files for batch domestic transfers

## v3.1.0 — March 2026
- **Vault Portfolios**: Launch of managed investment accounts for Premium members
- **Biometric Login**: Face ID and fingerprint authentication now available on all platforms
- **Card Freeze**: Instant card freezing and unfreezing through the mobile app

## v3.0.0 — January 2026
- **Complete Platform Redesign**: New dark-mode interface with improved accessibility
- **AI Support Assistant**: RAG-powered chatbot for instant policy lookups
- **Multi-Currency Accounts**: Hold and convert between USD, EUR, GBP, and CAD
- **API v2**: RESTful API with OAuth2 for Business tier integrations

## v2.8.0 — November 2025
- **International Transfers**: SWIFT and SEPA transfers now available for Premium and Business tiers
- **Statement Export**: Download statements in PDF, CSV, and OFX formats
- **Security Dashboard**: New unified view of all account security settings and login history
