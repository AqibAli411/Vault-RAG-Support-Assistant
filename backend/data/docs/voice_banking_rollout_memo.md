# Internal Memo: Voice Banking Rollout Timeline

Prepared by: Digital Channels PMO
Date: July 2026

The voice banking pilot is scheduled for August with employees and select beta customers.
If pilot metrics remain stable, public rollout starts in October with support for balance checks, transfer confirmations, and card lock requests.

Whisper-based transcription remains in QA and should not be announced externally until legal signs off on data retention language.
